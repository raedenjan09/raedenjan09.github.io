Rotoscope Project

Week by Week Progress

Week 1

Application used: After effects
Tools I've Used: Roto brush, puppet tool, and keyframes.

  • Roto brush
  • Puppet tool
  • Keyframes
In the firt week, I focused on learning the basics of rotoscoping using Adobe After Effects. I practiced using the Roto Brush tool to isolate subjects from their backgrounds and applied the Puppet Tool to animate specific parts of the subject. Keyframes were used to create smooth transitions and movements.

Week 2

Application used: Flipaclip
Tools I've Used: Pen tool, layers, and onion skinning.

  • Pen tool
  • Layers
  • Onion skinning
In the second week, I transitioned to using Flipaclip for frame-by-frame animation. I utilized the Pen Tool to draw and animate characters, while layers helped me manage different elements of the animation. Onion skinning was essential for ensuring smooth transitions between frames, allowing me to see previous and next frames while drawing.

Week 3

Application used: Flipaclip

  • Pen tool
  • Layers
  • Onion skinning
In the third week, I continued to refine my skills in Flipaclip. I focused on creating more complex animations by combining multiple layers and utilizing the Pen Tool for detailed drawings. The onion skinning feature was crucial for maintaining consistency in character movements and ensuring fluidity in the animation.
